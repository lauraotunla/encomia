Workflow — How to Use These Prompts

  • Step 1: Generate Prompt A in your image generator (Midjourney, Firefly, DALL-E, Ideogram). Set aspect ratio to 16:9, highest quality.
  • Step 2: Generate Prompt B — upload the Prompt A output as a reference/style image so lighting, angle, and materials stay consistent.
  • Step 3: Take both outputs into your video model (Runway Gen-3, Kling, Pika 2, Higgsfield). Upload A as the start frame, B as the end frame, then paste Prompt C as the motion description.
  • The reverse (end → start) often works equally well — a printed card flipping back to blank, or a banner rolling back up.
  • White backgrounds are intentional — they make compositing and web use much easier downstream.
